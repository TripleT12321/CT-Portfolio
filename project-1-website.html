Reflection

During the creation of this website, I learnt many new things. From barely knowing what a header and footer were to now being able to make everything I have done now really makes me feel accomplished as a person. Before I learnt how to code, I thought coding was a super complicated series of text that only the smartest could understand. Now I realise that code is just lines of text that are easy to understand once you get the hang of it. At the start of the creation of my website Hoop Dreams, I honestly had no idea what I wanted to do and what to do. I first copied my first demo of flexbox. However I wasn’t quite sure what I was doing. But with time I learnt how I could structure my website. When I was looking for inspiration I came upon the sidebar. Coding a sidebar seemed impossible but in reality, it was probably the most straightforward process ever. Technically I didn’t even do it due to the fact I just typed in how to code a sidebar into google. But it’s the thought that counts, you know. When I was considering the style of this website, I had no idea what fonts were good and what fonts were bad. It took me almost half an hour just to figure out what font would look good. Once I did find out what font looked good, it was just a waste of time. As my Michael Jordan background overwhelmed the text making the text look irrelevant. Eventually I settled on just a bold dominating bebas neue font. For the future, I would like to be able to figure out how to hide my sidebar on command. A button just being there to get rid of the sidebar would be awesome.
